# Prediction Narratives (Examples)

Use as templates to render investor- and operator-ready output. Bind from `predictions` and `features`.

## Narrative Template
Over the last {window}, {entity_name} exhibits {top_signal} with {signal_strength}% strength, driving a predicted {outcome} over {horizon} days at {confidence}% confidence. Top drivers: {drivers}. Recommended action: {action}. Rationale: {rationale}.

## Examples
1) SMB Distress
- Narrative: Over the last 14 days, Acme Logistics exhibits litigation mention velocity (72%) and negative hiring delta (58%), driving a predicted distress probability of 0.64 over 30 days at 81% confidence. Top drivers: legal filings, PR sentiment, hiring. Recommended action: Outreach to CFO with restructuring playbook. Rationale: peer outcomes in Q3.

2) Energy Conversion
- Narrative: Over the last 30 days, Lone Star Energy shows procurement delays and grid load spikes, predicting a 37% higher conversion likelihood for maintenance contracts over 90 days at 74% confidence. Top drivers: outage trends, permitting backlog, supplier risk. Action: prioritize enterprise AE intro within 48 hours.

3) Talent Drain Competitive Signal
- Narrative: Nimbus Tech exhibits elevated attrition signals and hiring freezes across core teams, predicting a 45% churn in enterprise SKUs over 60 days at 69% confidence. Drivers: job postings collapse, exec departures, Glassdoor trends. Action: competitive win-back sequence for top 10 accounts.
